Understanding Weight Issues

Comprehensive care for both overweight and underweight conditions

Overweight & Obesity

Being overweight or obese increases the risk of numerous health problems, including:

  • Heart disease and stroke
  • Type 2 diabetes
  • High blood pressure
  • Sleep apnea and breathing problems
  • Joint pain and mobility issues
  • Certain types of cancer

Our program addresses these risks through sustainable weight management strategies that focus on long-term health improvement rather than quick fixes.

Underweight Issues

Being underweight can also pose serious health risks, including:

  • Nutritional deficiencies
  • Weakened immune system
  • Osteoporosis and bone fractures
  • Fertility issues
  • Anemia and chronic fatigue
  • Developmental issues in younger individuals

We provide healthy weight gain programs that focus on building muscle mass and proper nutrition rather than empty calories.
